❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why can’t I train my own model?

Due to compute restrictions on this demo, we’ve pre-trained the model for you. For custom training, you can contact us or deploy the app on your own server!

Can I use my own dataset?

Currently, the app is optimized for the PJME_hourly.csv dataset. Stay tuned for updates, or reach out if you’d like to collaborate on adding support for custom datasets!

How accurate are the predictions?

The model achieves an RMSE of ~351 MW on the test set, which is typical for energy consumption forecasts. Accuracy depends on the quality and recency of the input data.

Can I deploy this app myself?

Yes! The app is open-source. Check out the GitHub repository for instructions on deploying it to platforms like Render, Heroku, or AWS.
